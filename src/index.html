Who voted for Article 13?

It's a dark day for the freedom of the internet. The EU parliament has passed the controversial copyright directive, including Article 13 (“upload filters”) and Article 11 (“link tax”). MEPs refused to even consider amendments. The results of the final vote: 348 in favor, 274 against.
